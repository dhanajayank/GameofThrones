  • " Winter is coming. "- Ned Stark, season one, episode one

  • " There is only one god and his name is Death, and there is only one thing we say to Death: 'Not today.' "- Syrio Forel, season one, episode six

  • " Swift as a deer. Quiet as a shadow. Fear cuts deeper than swords. Quick as a snake. Calm as still water. "
